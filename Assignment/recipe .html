chowmein

Chowmein is one of my favorite dish.Here is a delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 spegitte paket
  • 1 tbs garlic
  • 1 tbs ginger
  • 1 green chilli
  • 3 tbs chilli souce
  • 3 tbs soy souce

Method

  1. Cutting onion and capsicum
  2. Boil the noodles by adding hot water and salt to the frying pan
  3. After the noodles are boil,add 2tbs of oil,chilies,ginger & garlic to the frying pan
  4. Then add onion and capsicum
  5. Then add chilli souce,soy souce,salt and blackpepper
  6. Then mix these souces & add the noodles to the souces and leave for 2 minutes.After 2 minutes,it will be ready to eat
